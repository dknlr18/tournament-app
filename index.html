🏆 9-Player Tournament

3 groups of 3 · round-robin · then a 3-match playoff (Q1, Q2, Q3)

📺 Live scoreboard (read-only). Results are updated by the organiser.

1 Group Stage

Ranked by points (Win = 1, Loss = 0 — no draws), then by total points scored (PF). The top of each group qualifies.

2 Playoffs

The 3 group winners are seeded 1–3 (by points, then total points scored) and play three matches:

  • Q1 — Seed 1 v Seed 2. The winner goes straight to Q3; the loser drops into Q2 for a second chance.
  • Q2 — Loser of Q1 v Seed 3. The winner advances to Q3; the loser is eliminated.
  • Q3 (Final) — Winner of Q1 v Winner of Q2. The winner is the champion.

So the top two seeds get a second chance — one loss doesn't knock them out. Seed 3 must win twice.
