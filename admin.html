🛠️ Tournament Admin

Edit scores, then export data.js and push it to update the public site.

Workflow: edit scores below → Generate data.js → Download (or Copy) → replace data.js in the repo & commit. Your in-progress edits are auto-saved in this browser.

1 Group Stage

Ranked by points (Win = 2, Tie = 1, Loss = 0), then by total points scored (PF).

2 Playoffs

The 3 group winners are seeded 1–3 (by points, then total points scored) and play three matches:

  • Q1 — Seed 1 v Seed 2. Winner → Q3; loser drops to Q2.
  • Q2 — Loser of Q1 v Seed 3. Winner → Q3; loser is eliminated.
  • Q3 (Final) — Winner of Q1 v Winner of Q2. Winner is champion.

3 Publish

After downloading, replace data.js in your repo and commit/push. Only you can push, so only you can change the official scoreboard.
