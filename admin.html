How the tournament works

Group stage

  • 3 groups of 3, round-robin (3 matches per group).
  • Every match is best of 3 games; first to 2 games wins.
  • Games are to 11 points (win by 2 at deuce) — no draws. Match win = 1 point.
  • Ranked by points, then PF (most points scored), then PA (fewest conceded).

Playoffs

  • Q1 — Seed 1 v Seed 2. Winner → Q3; loser drops to Q2.
  • Q2 — Loser of Q1 v Seed 3. Loser is eliminated.
  • Q3 (Final) — Winner of Q1 v Winner of Q2 → champion.
